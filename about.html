About Stellar Communications Group

Delivering ERRCS Solutions that keep our communities safer.

Stellar Communications Group specializes in providing public safety radio ERRCS solutions. Our national expertise allows us to deploy in-building ERRCS solutions with 100% approval for Certificate of Occupancy or Temporary Certificate of Occupancy. Our dedicated team delivers turn-key ERRCS solutions that meet federal, state, and local requirements.
